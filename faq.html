Common Questions

Can't find your answer? Call us at +234 800 000 0000 or message us on WhatsApp.

Is BANSEK water NAFDAC certified?
+
Yes. BANSEK Pure Water is fully certified by NAFDAC. Our production facility meets all required hygiene and safety standards, and every batch is tested before packaging.
How do I place an order?
+
Fill in our online order form and click "Send Order on WhatsApp" — your order goes directly to us. You can also call +234 800 000 0000 or message us directly on WhatsApp.
Do you offer same-day delivery?
+
Yes. Orders placed before 12pm are eligible for same-day delivery within Lagos. Orders after 12pm are delivered the next business day. Call us to confirm availability in your area.
What areas do you deliver to?
+
We deliver across Lagos — including Ikeja, Lekki, Victoria Island, Surulere, Yaba, Ikorodu, and more. Contact us to confirm your specific location.
Is delivery free?
+
Delivery is free for orders of 10 bags or more within our standard coverage area. A small delivery fee may apply for smaller orders or locations outside our zone. We will always inform you before confirming.
How many sachets are in one bag?
+
Each bag contains 20 sachets of 500ml each — that is 10 litres of clean drinking water per bag.
Can I order for an event or party?
+
Absolutely. We handle bulk orders for events, parties, weddings, schools, churches, and corporate functions. Contact us in advance and we will offer special pricing for large orders.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept cash on delivery, bank transfer, and mobile payments. Account details are provided when you confirm your order.
How is the water purified?
+
Our water goes through multi-stage purification: filtration, reverse osmosis, UV sterilization, and ozonation. Every batch is tested before packaging to meet NAFDAC and WHO standards.
What if I receive damaged or bad water?
+
Contact us immediately at +234 800 000 0000 or via WhatsApp. We will arrange a replacement at no extra cost — your satisfaction is fully guaranteed.
